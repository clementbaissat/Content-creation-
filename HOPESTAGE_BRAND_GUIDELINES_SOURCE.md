## Summary

HopeStage is a lived experience and science driven mental health brand helping people living with bipolarity move from confusion to clarity. It combines psychoeducation, peer support, and practical tools to build stability and hope, starting in France and expanding internationally.

The brand is scientific but human, frank yet kind, friendly and respectful. It speaks directly to the reader using "you", avoids jargon, and focuses on small, actionable steps. The voice is warm, helpful, and plain spoken.

Visually, HopeStage represents "scientific hope" by balancing precision and humanity. The logo symbol, a rounded lowercase "g" forming a smiling person, reflects stability and recovery. The design uses soft geometry and a modern sans serif typeface to convey calm strength and trust.

The core color system centers on Elephant Green (`#154545`) for structure and text, Pale Goldenrod (`#E6E5B3`) for backgrounds, and Dull Orange (`#ED7534`) for highlights and calls to action, supported by a small secondary palette.

Clear rules are defined for logo spacing, sizing, background use, typography hierarchy, photography style, icon consistency, and data visualization. The goal is to ensure visual and verbal consistency across digital, print, and product applications.

In short, the brand blends evidence and empathy to create a coherent, recognizable, and trustworthy presence across all HopeStage touchpoints.
